## RUN THESE STEPS IN ORDER

### STEP 1: TECHNOLOGY FILTER (Quick Screen)

Score 1-5 on each dimension with evidence:
- Market Need — Is there validated demand?
- Market Size — Is the opportunity large enough?
- Technical Feasibility — Can it be built and scaled?
- Competitive Advantage — What's defensible?
- Team Fit — Can this team execute?
- IP Position — Is there protectable IP?
- Time to Market — How fast can it launch?
- Revenue Model — Is the business model clear?

Total score /40. Verdict: GO (32+) / CONDITIONAL (24-31) / PIVOT (16-23) / NO-GO (<16)

### STEP 2: MARKET SIZING

**Top-Down**: TAM → SAM → SOM with sources and methodology
**Bottom-Up**: [# customers] × [price] × [frequency] = revenue
**Reality Check**: Compare to 3 comparable companies at similar stage

### STEP 3: COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

Map on a 2x2 matrix:
- Direct competitors (same solution, same customer)
- Indirect competitors (different solution, same problem)
- Substitutes (different category, same budget)
- Status quo (doing nothing)

For top 3: positioning, strengths, weaknesses, funding, threat level

### STEP 4: 7-POWERS ANALYSIS

For each of Helmer's 7 Powers, assess:
- Do I have it now? (Y/N/Partial)
- Can I build it? (Y/N, with strategy)
- Does any competitor have it?

Recommend: Which 1-2 powers to prioritize building

### STEP 5: LEAN CANVAS

Build a complete canvas with all 9 boxes filled:
Problem | Solution | UVP | Unfair Advantage | Customer Segments
Key Metrics | Channels | Cost Structure | Revenue Streams

### STEP 6: ASSUMPTION TABLE

| Assumption | Risk Level | Validation Method | Priority |
List the top 10 assumptions. Rank by: if this is wrong, does the business fail?

### STEP 7: CUSTOMER DISCOVERY PLAN

For the #1 riskiest assumption:
- Hypothesis statement with success criteria
- 10-question interview guide (Mom Test compliant)
- Target: who to interview, how many, how to find them
- Analysis framework for synthesizing results
